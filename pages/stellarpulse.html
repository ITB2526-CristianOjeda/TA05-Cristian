StellarPulse

Deep Space Signal Analyzer • By Jesús & Cristian

What is this project about?

StellarPulse is an advanced simulator that analyzes fictional cosmic signals coming from different galaxies. The platform displays waves, patterns, intensities, and potential anomalies, allowing users to play as real astrophysicists.

The system determines if a signal could be natural, artificial, or unknown using a complex algorithm designed by us.

Why is it interesting?

It introduces users to the world of astrophysics simply, helping them understand how real space radio frequency detectors work.
It features detailed spectrum graphs, simulated sounds, and a massive history of over 12,000 generated signals. It is an ideal tool for both students and space enthusiasts.

The Story behind the code

The idea was born after watching documentaries about the search for extraterrestrial life (SETI). Jesús became interested in FRBs (Fast Radio Bursts) and Cristian in Artificial Intelligence. They decided to combine both interests and created a program capable of generating and analyzing fictional signals as if they came from distant galaxies.

They spent weeks tweaking the algorithm until they achieved a system that classified signals with surprising realism.

Strengths

  • Highly educational and interactive.
  • Explains complex concepts with simple graphics.
  • Large simulated database available.
  • Scalable architecture for new galaxies.

Current Challenges

  • Sluggishness when simulating many simultaneous signals.
  • Does not allow signal comparison between users (yet).
  • Requires a good connection for real-time features.
